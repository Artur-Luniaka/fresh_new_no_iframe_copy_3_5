Privacy Notice

Data Collection

We collect essential data to enhance your soccer gaming experience, including match results, player stats, and device information.

How We Use Your Data

Game Progress

We store your tournament progress, unlocked skills, and career stats to maintain your soccer journey.

Performance Data

Anonymous match statistics help us improve header mechanics and balance gameplay.

Device Information

Basic device data ensures optimal soccer gameplay performance on your system.

Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your soccer data:

  • Encrypted storage of player profiles and stats
  • Regular security updates for our soccer servers
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure backup of your football career

Your Rights

As a soccer player, you have the right to:

  • Access your match history and stats
  • Request deletion of your player data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Export your football career progress

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions about your soccer data, contact our support team through the Support page.

Policy Updates
