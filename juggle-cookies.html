Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you play our soccer game. They help maintain your game progress, preferences, and enhance your football experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Save your match progress and career stats
  • Store high scores and achievements
  • Remember your control preferences
  • Analyze gameplay to improve soccer mechanics
  • Secure your player account

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for core soccer gameplay. These ensure proper saving of matches, tournaments, and player progression.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how players interact with our football game to improve header mechanics and gameplay balance.

Functionality Cookies

Remember your preferred controls, team selections, and visual settings for optimal soccer matches.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your soccer career progress, saved teams, and some gameplay features may not function properly.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as we add new football features. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.
